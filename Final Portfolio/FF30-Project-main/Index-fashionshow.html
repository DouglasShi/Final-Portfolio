Fashion Show

According to the most famous fashion designer Karl Lagerfield, elegance is not the pursuit of extravagant outfits, but an attitude of life. Therefore, for our FF30 movie festival project, we invited random students in the campus of NYUAD to confidently present their outfits of the day on our non-existent catwalk. Through this project, we want to spread the idea that elegance and fashion has nothing to do with who you are, where you are, and what are you wearing.
